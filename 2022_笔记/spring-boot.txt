结合websocket的例子
https://cloud.tencent.com/developer/article/1830998

配置如下，让spring-boot工程打包成一个能直接运行的大jar包
  <build>
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
        <artifactId>spring-boot-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <executions>
          <execution>
            <goals>
              <goal>repackage</goal>
            </goals>
          </execution>
        </executions>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
  </build>
